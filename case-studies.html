Fortune 500 Manufacturing Success Story

Manufacturing

The Challenge

A global manufacturing leader with operations across 25 countries was struggling with data fragmentation across 15+ legacy systems. Their monthly reporting process required 40 hours of manual work, involved 8 different analysts, and often resulted in inconsistent KPI calculations across departments.

Our Solution

We implemented a comprehensive Power BI solution with:

  • Unified data model integrating all 15 legacy systems
  • Automated ETL processes reducing manual intervention
  • Interactive dashboards for real-time monitoring
  • Self-service analytics capabilities for business users

Implementation Timeline

  • Weeks 1-3: Data assessment and architecture design
  • Weeks 4-8: Data integration and model development
  • Weeks 9-12: Dashboard creation and user training
  • Weeks 13-16: Testing, refinement, and rollout

Long-term Impact

Twelve months post-implementation, the client reported sustained improvements in operational efficiency, better decision-making capabilities, and increased user adoption rates of 85% across the organization.
